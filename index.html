A Store Built on Faith, Knowledge & Service

Fatima Book Depot is a trusted Islamic retail store located near Jama Masjid, Tuni. We provide authentic Islamic books, Quran Majeed, Panj Surah, prayer essentials, attars, traditional clothing, gift collections and community products.

Our goal is to make Islamic knowledge and essential products easily accessible while maintaining honesty, trust, quality and service. Every product we offer is carefully selected to support learning, worship and daily Islamic living.

Hafiz Md Kabiruddin Razvi

Founder & Proprietor | Imam – Jama Masjid Tuni

Hafiz Md Kabiruddin Razvi is a respected Hafiz-e-Quran, Imam, Islamic educator and community guide who has been serving the people of Tuni for more than 10 years. Known for his dedication towards Deen, Islamic education and community welfare, he has guided countless individuals and families through his teachings, counseling and service.
